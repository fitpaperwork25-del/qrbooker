QRServe — Brand Guidelines
===========================
Last updated: May 15, 2026
Platform owner: Michael A. Etbarekh (fitpaperwork25@gmail.com)


LOGO
----
Layout: Horizontal — QR mark on the left, wordmark and tagline on the right
QR Mark: Three finder patterns (top-left, top-right, bottom-left) + data dots (bottom-right)
Wordmark: "QRServe" — "QR" in white, "Serve" in gold
Font: Arial Black, 64px, weight 900, letter-spacing -2
Tagline: "SCAN · ORDER · SERVE" — bright light grey, letter-spacing 4
Underline: Thin gold line beneath the tagline
Gap: Clear breathing room between QR mark and wordmark


COLORS
------
Background (primary):   #080808  — near black
Gold accent:            #E8C547  — brand gold, used on QR mark, "Serve", underline
White text:             #F0EDE8  — warm white, used on "QR" wordmark
Tagline text:           #C8C4BC  — light grey, used on tagline
Muted text:             #666666  — secondary/muted information
Dark surface:           #111108  — slightly lighter than background, used inside QR mark
Border/divider:         rgba(255,255,255,0.08) — subtle borders


TYPOGRAPHY
----------
Primary font:     Arial Black (headings, wordmark, bold UI)
Secondary font:   Arial / sans-serif (body, tagline, UI labels)
Wordmark size:    64px, weight 900
Tagline size:     14px, weight 400, letter-spacing 4
Body text:        16px, weight 400, line-height 1.7


LOGO USAGE RULES
----------------
- Always use on dark (#080808) background
- Never place on white or light backgrounds
- Minimum clear space: equal to the height of the "Q" on all sides
- Do not stretch, rotate, or recolor the logo
- Do not use "QR" and "Serve" in the same color
- The gold underline beneath the tagline is part of the logo — do not remove it
- QR mark and wordmark must always appear together — do not use separately


TAGLINE
-------
Primary: SCAN · ORDER · SERVE
Use case: All brand materials, email headers, print collateral, QR deployment kits


BRAND VOICE
-----------
- Direct and confident
- No fluff — every word earns its place
- Hospitality-forward — warm but professional
- Empowering to restaurant owners and staff
