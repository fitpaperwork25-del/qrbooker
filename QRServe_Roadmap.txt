QRServe v3 — Product Roadmap
=============================
Last updated: May 15, 2026


IMMEDIATE PRIORITIES (before first paying client)
--------------------------------------------------
- Close Red Sea (Yohannes) signup
- Close Snelling Cafe (Afeworki) signup
- Deploy Bole Restaurant
- Send welcome deployment packet to each new client


SHORT TERM (after first 3 paying clients)
------------------------------------------
- Plan feature gating — enforce Starter/Pro/Enterprise limits
- Full e2e automated system test
- Menu add/edit from owner dashboard
- Order cancel analytics improvements


MEDIUM TERM
-----------
- Multilingual scan page
  Languages: Spanish, French, Italian, German (Latin script — one session)
  Then: Arabic (RTL), Amharic (Ethiopic script — separate session)
  Trigger: when a client's customers are not English-speaking
  Tech: react-i18next, Noto Sans Ethiopic for Amharic, RTL layout for Arabic

- Admin page — plan feature gating enforcement
  Starter: location limit, no staff management
  Pro: up to 5 locations, staff management
  Enterprise: unlimited, white label, custom domain

- Automated welcome email (Resend)
  Triggered on registration
  Includes: branded email, deployment kit, QR code, quick start, dashboard CTA
  Design: dark #080808, gold #E8C547, QRServe logo in header


LONG TERM
---------
- White label option (Enterprise plan)
- Custom domain per restaurant (Enterprise)
- Native mobile app for staff kitchen view
- Loyalty/rewards system for customers
- Integration with POS systems
- Multi-location analytics dashboard
- Stay Africa integration (hotel room ordering)


NOTES
-----
- Multilingual: do not build until a client requests it
- Feature gating: do not enforce until you have clients on different plans
- All new features should be validated by at least one client before building
